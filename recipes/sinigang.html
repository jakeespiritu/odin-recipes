Pork Sinigang

Description

A sour and savory soup dish that will melt your heart on a cold day!

Ingredients

  • Pork
  • Lemon
  • Ginger
  • Onions
  • Lettuce
  • Salt
  • Sugar
  • Tomatoes
  • Fish Sauce

Steps

  1. Boil pork in water and take off the scum.
  2. Sear pork in pot and put aside.
  3. Sweat onions and saut√© with ginger.
  4. Add meat back in and season with salt, sugar, lemon, and fish sauce
  5. Put in tomatoes and melt until a sauce is made
  6. Add in water; boil and simmer until meat is cooked
  7. Add lettuce and simmer for another 15 minutes then serve with rice
