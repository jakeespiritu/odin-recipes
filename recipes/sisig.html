Pork Sisig

Description

A crispy and deliciously sizzling pork dish that is both sour and savory!

Ingredients

  • Pork Belly
  • Chili Peppers
  • Kalamansi
  • Red Onions
  • Soy Sauce
  • Ground Black Pepper
  • Chicken Liver
  • Mayonnaise (optional)

Steps

  1. Slice pork belly and chicken liver into minced pieces
  2. Sear pork and chicken liver together in set aside
  3. Saut√© red onions and chili peppers in a cast iron
  4. Add kalamansi juice, soy sauce, ground black pepper then place both meats in the same pan
  5. Serve with rice while cast iron is still sizzling and top with mayonnaise
