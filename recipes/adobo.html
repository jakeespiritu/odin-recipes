Chicken Adobo

Description

The National Dish of the Philippines, Chicken Adobo is a savory and sweet dish that will tantalize your taste buds!

Ingredients

  • Chicken Thighs
  • Soy Sauce
  • White Vinegar
  • Onions
  • Garlic
  • Peppercorns
  • Sugar
  • Bay Leaves

Steps

  1. Combine ingredients and marinade chicken overnight
  2. Sear chicken in oil and set aside

  4. Add sugar and meat back in
